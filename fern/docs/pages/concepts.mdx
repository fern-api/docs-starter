---
title: Petstore Docs | Concepts
description: Learn about the key concepts of the Petstore API. 
---

**Pet**: A pet is an animal that can be bought from the store. It has a name, a category, a status, and a list of photo URLs.

**Store**: A store has a collection of pets. It is responsible for managing the inventory of pets. Users place orders for pets from the store.

**User**: A user is a person who buys pets from the store. Also known as pet parents.
