## Basic guidelines

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Core principles">
1. Check soil moisture before watering
2. Match plants to available light conditions
3. Use diluted fertilizer during growing season
4. Allow time for plants to adjust to new environments
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Beginner-friendly plants">
If you're new to plant care, these species are forgiving and adaptable:

- **Pothos** (*Epipremnum aureum*): Tolerates low light and inconsistent watering
- **Snake plant** (*Sansevieria trifasciata*): Survives neglect and low light
- **Spider plant** (*Chlorophytum comosum*): Adapts to various conditions and propagates easily
- **ZZ plant** (*Zamioculcas zamiifolia*): Drought-tolerant with minimal light requirements
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Common issues">

- Overwatering causes most plant failures
- Insufficient drainage leads to root rot
- Inadequate light results in weak, leggy growth
- Fluorinated tap water can damage sensitive species
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
