---
title: thrust::complex
description: "`complex` is the Thrust equivalent to `std::complex`."
---

`complex` is the Thrust equivalent to `std::complex`.

It is functionally identical to it, but can also be used in device code which `std::complex` currently cannot.

```cpp showLineNumbers={false}
#include <thrust/complex.h>
```

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Template parameters">

<ParamField path="T" type="typename">
The type used to hold the real and imaginary parts. Should be `float` or `double`. Others types are not supported. embed:rst:leading-asterisk
*     .. versionadded:: 2.2.0
*
</ParamField>

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
